Privacy Policy

We are committed to securing your data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details exactly what information we collect, how it is processed, and how you can request its deletion.

1. Data We Collect & Store

To provide persistent user configurations, watchlists, and feedback, we store specific, non-personally identifiable variables inside a secure, thread-isolated SQLite relational database:

  • User Identification: We store your unique Discord Snowflake ID and Username when configuring budget limits, saving watches, or submitting feedback.
  • Persistent Watchlists: When running /track, we log target items, trigger values, and alert directions.
  • User Configurations: Preferences such as custom /budget thresholds are kept.
  • Debug Reports: Any /debug query records usernames, issue categories, description fields, and a timestamp.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

We never collect or request your Jagex Account Credentials, passwords, or bank PINs. We do not read, log, monitor, or store your private text messages. The Bot only processes commands explicitly targeting its registered structures. We do not collect IP addresses or real-world personal information.

3. Data Retention & Security

All data is stored locally inside an indexed SQLite database configured in Write-Ahead Logging (WAL) mode. Parameterized inputs prevent SQL injection vectors. Debug reports and watchlists are retained until manually purged, while pricing snapshots are automatically pruned after 30 days.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Under Discord’s Developer Policy and global privacy standards, you have the right to request the complete deletion of your data at any time. To remove watchlists, run /watchlist or use /track in reverse. To request a complete database expunge of your recorded IDs, contact the developer directly and the data rows will be deleted within 48 hours.
