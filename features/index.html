Browse the Features

Discover the full suite of modules available within Count Check, engineered to optimize OSRS progression, combat tracking, and financial portfolios.

Quantitative Portfolio Sizing

/flip

Generates highly diversified, capital-calibrated recommendation portfolios based on your selected strategy horizon (Standard, Fast Commodities, or Overnight long-holds) and operating budget.

/f2p

Formulates members=False portfolios optimized specifically for tight, low-liquidity Free-to-Play margin environments.

/budget

Sets your personal cash limit profile (supports standard shorthand notation like 500k, 20m, 1.5b) to automatically scale portfolio sizing calculations.

Market Analytics & Arbitrage

/analyze

Compiles a comprehensive, 3-page real-time dossier evaluating price price channel boundaries, 14-day relative positions (RCPI), corrective risks, and competitor undercut timelines.

/market movers

Displays top directional price shifts (combining trend lines with item icons) or volume-velocity breakout momentum.

/potion

Identifies profitable decanting loops (buying 3-dose potions, decanting via Bob Barter, and liquidating 4-dose potion volumes) calibrated directly against local trade limits and tax deductions.

/sets

Analyzes piece-by-piece packing loops compared to completed boxed sets to identify profitable Grand Exchange packaging spreads.

/alch

Scans the active database for tax-free High Alchemy margins, calculating dynamic nature-rune costs against in-game coin-conversion floors.

/compare

Displays a side-by-side comparative ledger contrasting margins, ROI, and relative correction risks of two distinct assets.

/graph

Compiles and renders historical price paths plotted against 1-hour transaction volume bars.

Symmetrical Game Simulators

/kill

Simulates killing any OSRS boss up to 10,000 times, delivering high-speed loot grid visuals and a calculated market valuation breakdown.

/open

Opens minigame caskets (such as Guardians of the Rift, Soul Wars, or Barbarian Assault), utilizing customized server headers.

/clue

Simulates opening clue caskets (Beginner to Master), rendering color-themed reward sheets and casket icons.

/pet

Continually rolls exact drop rates until obtaining a pet, generating derived resource breakdowns, gained XP counts, and cumulative GP valuations.

Player Progression Metrics

/temple stats

Queries Temple OSRS and renders a classic highscores sheet complete with combat levels, overall levels, and boss kills.

/temple pets

Tracks unlocked pets against standard registries, shifting the embed color from yellow to green upon total completion (69/69).

/temple clog

Renders categorised collection logs, highlighting achieved goals in green or yellow depending on completion rates.

/temple update

Submits secure POST requests to Temple's datapoint system, forcing fresh Jagex profile parses.
